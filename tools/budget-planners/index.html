Budgeting Tips for Beginners

Building a budget doesn't have to be complicated. Here's a simple framework to get started:

  • 50/30/20 Rule: Allocate 50% of income to needs, 30% to wants, and 20% to savings and debt repayment
  • Pay yourself first: Set up automatic transfers to savings on payday — before you have a chance to spend
  • Track every dollar: Use our savings calculators to monitor specific goals like your emergency fund or vacation fund
  • Start small: Even $50 a month adds up. Consistency beats intensity every time
  • Review monthly: Check your progress at the end of each month and adjust as needed
