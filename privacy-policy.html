KoalaSave ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website at koalasave.com (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

KoalaSave（"我们"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了当您使用我们的网站 koalasave.com（"服务"）时，我们如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

使用本服务即表示您同意本政策中描述的实践。

1. Information We Do NOT Collect / 我们不会收集的信息

KoalaSave does NOT collect, store, upload, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal data to use the savings calculator.

All financial goals, target amounts, savings progress, and calculator inputs you enter are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data never leaves your computer or mobile device unless you choose to share it (e.g., by copying a share URL).

KoalaSave 不会收集、存储、上传或传输任何个人身份信息（PII）。您无需注册账号、提供姓名、电子邮件地址或任何其他个人数据即可使用储蓄计算器。

您输入的所有储蓄目标、金额、进度和计算器数据仅存储在您设备浏览器的 localStorage 中。除非您主动选择分享（例如通过复制分享链接），这些数据永远不会离开您的计算机或移动设备。

✅ We do NOT have a server database. We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share your data with third parties. All data stays on YOUR device.

✅ 我们没有服务器数据库。我们不出售您的数据，不与第三方共享您的数据。所有数据都保留在您的设备上。

2. LocalStorage (Browser Storage) / 浏览器本地存储

Our Service uses the browser's localStorage technology to store your data, not traditional cookies. This is a standard web storage mechanism that allows websites to save data locally within your browser.

What we store in localStorage:

  • Your savings goals and their details (goal name, target amount, saved amount, monthly contribution)
  • Your current route selection (which savings tool you last used)
  • Calculator state preferences

Important: localStorage data is tied to your browser profile on your specific device. Clearing your browser's site data or cache will permanently delete your savings goals. We recommend regularly noting down important goal information separately.

本服务使用浏览器的 localStorage 技术存储您的数据，而非传统的 Cookie。这是一种标准的 Web 存储机制，允许网站在您的浏览器中本地保存数据。

我们在 localStorage 中存储的内容：

  • 您的储蓄目标及详情（目标名称、目标金额、已存金额、每月储蓄额）
  • 您当前的路由选择（您上次使用的储蓄工具）
  • 计算器状态偏好

重要提示：localStorage 数据与您特定设备上的浏览器配置文件绑定。清除浏览器的网站数据或缓存将永久删除您的储蓄目标。建议您定期在其他地方备份重要目标信息。

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately — though we emphasize that our system is technically incapable of collecting such data.

遵守美国的 《儿童在线隐私保护法》(COPPA) 和 GDPR 第 8 条（要求 16 岁以下儿童获得父母同意），我们不会也无法从任何用户收集个人信息，无论年龄大小，因为我们的架构不包含任何服务器端数据收集功能。

  • 删除权：由于所有数据都存储在您浏览器的本地存储中，您可以随时通过清除浏览器中 koalasave.com 的 localStorage 或网站数据来删除数据。

To remove all data stored by KoalaSave, simply clear your browser's localStorage for the koalasave.com domain in your browser settings.
