Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

KoalaSave ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website at koalasave.com (the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

KoalaSave does NOT collect, store, upload, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) through the savings calculator. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal data to use the savings calculator. The only exception is our optional Contact page: if you choose to fill out that form, the name, email address, and message you submit are sent to us via a third-party email service so we can respond to you (see Section 3e below).

All financial goals, target amounts, savings progress, and calculator inputs you enter are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data never leaves your computer or mobile device unless you choose to share it (e.g., by copying a share URL).

We do NOT have a server database. We do NOT sell your data. We do NOT share your data with third parties. All data stays on YOUR device.

2. LocalStorage (Browser Storage)

Our Service uses the browser's localStorage technology to store your data, not traditional cookies. This is a standard web storage mechanism that allows websites to save data locally within your browser.

What we store in localStorage:

  • Your savings goals and their details (goal name, target amount, saved amount, monthly contribution)
  • Your current route selection (which savings tool you last used)
  • Calculator state preferences

Important: localStorage data is tied to your browser profile on your specific device. Clearing your browser's site data or cache will permanently delete your savings goals. We recommend regularly noting down important goal information separately.

3. Third-Party Services

While KoalaSave itself does not collect any data, we may use third-party services that may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies:

a. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies (or similar technologies) to collect anonymous usage data such as page views, session duration, and browser type. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. We use Google Analytics in its most privacy-friendly configuration with IP anonymization enabled.

On pages where analytics is used, we display a cookie consent banner that lets you Accept or Reject non-essential cookies. Analytics and advertising cookies default to a denied state and only activate once you choose to Accept; your choice is remembered for future visits and can be changed by clearing your browser's site data.

b. Google AdSense

We may display advertisements via Google AdSense. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history and interests. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

c. Affiliate Links

Our website may contain affiliate links to third-party products or services (e.g., bank accounts, travel booking sites). Clicking these links may result in a commission paid to us at no additional cost to you. These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

d. Cloudflare

Our website is hosted and served through Cloudflare, which may process minimal technical data (such as IP addresses) for security and performance optimization purposes. Cloudflare's privacy policy is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

e. Contact Form (Brevo)

If you use our Contact page to send us a message, the name, email address, and message you provide are transmitted to Brevo (a third-party transactional email provider) solely to deliver your message to us and allow us to reply. Brevo's privacy policy is available at brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy. We do not use this information for any other purpose, and it is never used to power the savings calculator itself.

4. International Data Transfers

The third-party services described above (including Google, Cloudflare, and Brevo) are based in, or operate servers located in, the United States and other countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and United Kingdom. Where your data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, these providers rely on recognized transfer mechanisms such as the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, its UK Extension, or Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission. We encourage you to review each provider's own privacy policy (linked above) for details on their specific safeguards.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR)

KoalaSave is a general audience website and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under the age of 16 in the European Union / UK). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and Article 8 of the GDPR (which requires parental consent for children under 16), we do not and cannot collect personal information from any user, regardless of age, because our architecture does not include any server-side data collection capabilities.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data to us, please contact us immediately — though we emphasize that our system is technically incapable of collecting such data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal data is collected about you. As stated above, we collect no personal data.
  • Right to Delete: Since all data is stored locally in your browser, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data for koalasave.com.
  • Right to Opt Out: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, and opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing (CCPA): We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration, and we do not engage in cross-context behavioral advertising as defined by the CCPA.

Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, most data subject access requests under GDPR/CCPA are inherently fulfilled: there is simply no data to access, rectify, or transfer.

7. Data Retention

Your data remains in your browser's localStorage indefinitely until you or your browser's storage management system removes it. We have no ability to access, modify, or delete your localStorage data. To remove all data stored by KoalaSave, simply clear your browser's localStorage for the koalasave.com domain in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
