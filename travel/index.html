🌍 5 Tips to Save for Your Dream Trip Faster
  • Open a dedicated travel fund — Keep your trip savings separate from everyday accounts so you're less tempted to dip into them.
  • Automate monthly transfers — Set up an automatic transfer on payday. You can't spend what you don't see.
  • Cut one subscription — The average American wastes $21/month on unused subscriptions. Redirect that cash to your travel fund.
  • Pick up a side gig — Even $200/month from freelance work, dog walking, or selling unused items makes a huge difference.
  • Use a high-yield savings account — Earn 4-5% APY on your travel fund instead of letting it sit in a 0.01% checking account.

❓ Travel Savings FAQ
How much should I budget for a trip?
A typical 5-7 day international trip ranges from $1,500-5,000 per person depending on your destination, season, and travel style. Use this calculator to set a realistic target based on your specific dream trip.
How can I save for a vacation faster?
Open a dedicated travel savings fund, automate monthly transfers, cut discretionary spending, or pick up a side gig. Use the "What If" slider above to see how increasing your monthly savings accelerates your trip. Even an extra $50/month can shave months off your timeline.
How far ahead should I start saving for a trip?
Ideally 6-12 months ahead. This gives you enough time to build a comfortable travel fund without stress. The calculator above shows exactly how much to save each month based on your target date and budget. Starting early also means better deals on flights and accommodation.
Is this travel budget calculator really free?
Yes, completely free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden fees. All data stays in your browser locally. KoalaSave is supported by ads and affiliate referrals, never by paywalls.
How do I use this travel savings calculator?
Enter your trip budget, how much you've already saved, how much you can save monthly, and when you want to travel. The calculator instantly shows your progress, how much you need per month, and how long it'll take. Save multiple goals to compare different trip ideas!
