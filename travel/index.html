❓ Maldives Travel FAQ

How much does a Maldives trip cost?
A 5-7 day Maldives trip typically costs $2,500-5,000 per person including flights, overwater bungalow accommodation, meals, and activities. The mid-range budget (around $3,500-4,000) gets you a solid experience with a mix of local island stays and resort visits. Setting a realistic budget is the first step toward paradise.
How can I save for a Maldives vacation faster?
Open a dedicated travel savings fund, automate monthly transfers, cut discretionary spending, pick up a side gig, or use the "What If" slider above to see how increasing your monthly savings accelerates your trip. Even an extra $50/month can shave months off your timeline.
When is the best time to book Maldives?
The dry season (November-April) offers the best weather with sunny skies and calm seas. Book flights 3-6 months in advance and hotels 6-12 months ahead for the best rates on overwater villas. Shoulder months (November and April) offer a sweet spot of good weather and lower prices.
