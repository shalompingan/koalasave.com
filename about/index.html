Our Story

KoalaSave was born from a simple frustration: why is something as important as savings planning buried behind banking portals, spreadsheets, and paid apps?

Most savings calculators are either too basic to be useful or locked behind subscription paywalls. We wanted something different — a tool that's genuinely helpful, completely free, and respects your privacy.

Named after the koala — a creature that embodies preparedness without anxiety — KoalaSave helps you build your financial safety net one step at a time. Whether you're saving for an emergency fund, a dream vacation, or your first home, we're here to make the process clear and motivating.

Our Privacy Philosophy

We take your privacy seriously — not as a marketing slogan, but as a fundamental design principle:

  • Zero server-side storage. All your data lives in your browser's localStorage. We can't see it, we can't access it, and we can't lose it.
  • No account required. Start saving immediately — no signup, no email, no password.
  • Minimal analytics. We use Google Analytics with anonymized IPs and Consent Mode v2. No personal data is sent.
  • Transparent monetization. The site is supported by AdSense ads and affiliate links. We clearly label all affiliate content. Our tools remain free regardless.
